Places Lived

Before making this map, I thought it would be a little more exciting. Then I realized I apparently have an aversion to anything outside of the Rust Belt region. I do have good coverage of the I-96 corridor through Michigan, though.

This map was created using the Leaflet API.


Kenya Fieldwork

While working on my dissertation, I collected survey data from water management groups around Mount Kenya. Their center points are shown on the map. The water projects are colored according to the catchment authority (known as a WRUA) to which they belong.

This map was created using the Leaflet API.


State CO2 Emissions

Using data from the U.S. Energy Information Administration, I've mapped out per capita energy-related CO2 emissions for each state. 'Energy-related CO2 emissions' refers to emissions released at the location where energy is consumed.

This map was created using the Leaflet API.


Orion Township Safe Routes to Schools

The Charter Township of Orion, MI is participating in the Safe Routes to Schools program. The program provides funding for infrastructure enhancements to create safe, walkable routes to schools. Click on the image on the left to view the enhancements identified by Orion Township.

This application was created using Esri's Experience Builder and Web AppBuilder.
